🔒 ASC Screenshot Creator processes all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with servers or third parties.

1. Information We Collect

ASC Screenshot Creator accesses the following data solely to provide app functionality:

  • Screenshots & Photos — The app accesses screenshots and photos from your photo library only when you explicitly select them for editing or composing App Store Connect marketing images.
  • App Settings & Preferences — Device-specific settings such as your preferred device frame, background color, and template selections are stored locally on your device to remember your preferences between sessions.

No other data is collected. We do not access contacts, location, browsing history, identifiers, health data, or any other personal information.

2. How We Use Your Data

All data accessed by ASC Screenshot Creator is used exclusively to provide the app's core functionality — creating and editing App Store Connect screenshots. Specifically:

  • Photos and screenshots you select are rendered within the app's editing canvas on your device.
  • Your settings and preferences are saved locally to preserve your customization choices.
  • Finished compositions are exported back to your photo library only when you choose to save them.

🚫 No Data Leaves Your Device

ASC Screenshot Creator does not transmit, upload, or send any user data — including screenshots, photos, settings, or device identifiers — to any server, cloud service, or remote endpoint. All processing occurs entirely on-device.

3. No Third-Party Analytics

ASC Screenshot Creator does not include any third-party analytics SDKs, tracking frameworks, or advertising networks. We do not use services such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Flurry, Mixpanel, Facebook SDK, or any equivalent tool. There is no telemetry, no usage tracking, and no crash reporting data sent to third parties.

4. No Advertising

ASC Screenshot Creator contains no advertisements and does not include any ad SDKs or frameworks. We do not serve ads, collect advertising identifiers, or participate in any advertising networks.

5. No Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any user data to any third party for any purpose. Because we do not collect data on our servers, there is nothing to share. Your data remains entirely under your control on your device.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Since ASC Screenshot Creator does not collect or store any data on external servers, there is no data retention policy to enforce on our end. All data remains on your device:

  • To delete your screenshots/photos: Remove them from your device's photo library.
  • To reset app preferences: Delete the app from your device, which removes all locally stored settings.

7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected — We collect no personal information beyond the photos and settings you provide locally within the app.
  • Delete personal information — All data is stored locally on your device; deleting the app removes all app data.
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information — We do not sell any personal information.
  • Non-discrimination — We do not discriminate against consumers who exercise their privacy rights.

Because ASC Screenshot Creator does not collect, transmit, or store data on any server, these rights are inherently satisfied. If you have any CCPA-related questions, please contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

ASC Screenshot Creator is rated 4+ on the App Store and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or any age). The app does not include any features that would collect, transmit, or share data from children. Since no data leaves the device, children's privacy is fully protected by design.

9. Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Since all data processing occurs locally on your device and no data is transmitted over the internet, the risk of data interception or unauthorized access is minimized. We recommend using your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID) to protect your data.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any changes.
